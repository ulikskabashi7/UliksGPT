More Information

This page contains additional details about our chatbot.

The More Info page helps users understand what a chatbot is and how this chatbot works. A chatbot is a computer program that communicates with users through text and answers questions automatically. This chatbot uses artificial intelligence to understand questions and provide helpful and clear responses. It can be used to get information, learn new things, and receive quick assistance at any time. The page also explains that the chatbot is not a human and has limits, helping users know what to expect. Overall, the More Info page gives users simple and useful information to feel confident using the chatbot.
